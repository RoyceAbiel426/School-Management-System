## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. **Get Your Login Credentials**

   - Your administrator will provide:
     - **Email**: Usually `studentID@school.edu` or your personal email
     - **Password**: A temporary password (you'll change this on first login)

2. **Access the System**

   - Open your web browser
   - Go to: `https://your-school.edupro.com`
   - Or use the link your school provided

3. **Enter Your Credentials**

   ```
   Email: [Your email or student ID]
   Password: [Your password]

   [Login Button]
   ```

4. **First-Time Login**

   - You'll be asked to change your password
   - Create a strong password:
     - At least 8 characters
     - Mix of uppercase and lowercase
     - Include numbers and special characters (@, #, $, etc.)
   - Example: `MySchool@2025`

5. **Stay Logged In** (Optional)
   - Check "Remember Me" to stay logged in
   - âš ï¸ Only on your personal device!
